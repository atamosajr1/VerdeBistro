Why Verde?

A Dining Experience Worth Savoring

We believe great food starts long before it reaches your plate — it starts with how we source, prepare, and serve it.

Seasonal Ingredients

Our menu rotates every 6–8 weeks to celebrate what's freshest from our network of 18 local farms across Central Texas.

Open Kitchen

Watch our chefs at work. Our open kitchen design lets you see every dish prepared from scratch with the same care every time.

Craft Cocktails & Wine

Curated natural wines, craft beers from Austin breweries, and house cocktails made with garden-grown herbs and local spirits.

Zero-Waste Kitchen

We compost 100% of our food waste, source packaging from recycled materials, and are proud to be carbon-neutral since 2022.

Specialty Coffee Bar

Our in-house café opens at 7am daily featuring single-origin espresso, cold brew, and seasonal lattes from our roaster partners.

Private Events

Host your next birthday, anniversary, or corporate dinner in our private dining room. Accommodates up to 40 guests.

Our Story

Born from a Love of Good Food & Community

Verde Bistro & Café began as a small pop-up market stall run by Chef Maria Delgado and her partner Carlos. What started as weekend brunch service quickly grew into one of Austin's most beloved neighborhood restaurants.

We built Verde on a simple belief: food tastes better when you know where it came from. Every ingredient on our menu has a name, a farm, and a story — and we love sharing that story with you.

Today, Verde is home to a team of 24 passionate cooks, baristas, and hosts who show up every day to make sure your experience is exceptional.
